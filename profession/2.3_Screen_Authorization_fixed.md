## KEY PERMISSION RULES

### Admin Role
- Full system configuration access
- User and role management
- Branch management
- Website content management
- Cannot perform sales/warehouse operations
- Views all system logs

### Owner Role
- **Cross-branch access**: Views data from ALL branches
- Approves large expenses
- Configures customer tier thresholds
- Cannot directly operate POS
- Cannot modify system logs
- Full reporting access

### Store Manager Role
- **Branch-restricted**: Only assigned branch data
- Approves returns/exchanges
- Approves inventory adjustments
- Can cancel orders
- Manages branch-level pricing
- Cannot view other branches

### Sales Staff Role
- **Branch-restricted**: Only assigned branch
- Cannot cancel completed orders
- **Limited discount authority**: Requires manager approval for large discounts
- Views own sales data only
- Cannot view financial reports
- Real-time inventory check required
- Cannot sell beyond available stock

### Warehouse Staff Role
- Manages import/export operations
- Cannot directly adjust inventory (requires manager approval)
- Cannot view financial reports
- Cannot perform sales operations
- Inventory adjustments require manager approval

### Guest Role
- No login required
- View-only website access
- Cannot purchase online
- Cannot access admin system
- Cannot view inventory levels

## CUSTOMER TIER SYSTEM

| Tier | Total Spending | Discount % | Benefits |
|------|----------------|------------|----------|
| **Regular** | < 5,000,000 VND | 0% | None |
| **Silver** | 5,000,000 - 20,000,000 VND | 3% | Priority return/exchange |
| **Gold** | 20,000,000 - 50,000,000 VND | 5% | Priority + birthday gift |
| **VIP** | > 50,000,000 VND | 10% | All benefits + credit sales |

**Note**: Thresholds and percentages are configurable by Owner

---

## BUSINESS RULES

1. **Multi-store**: All data tagged with store_id
2. **RBAC**: Role-Based Access Control enforced
3. **Real-time inventory**: Updates after sales, imports, transfers, returns
4. **Automatic tier upgrade**: Based on cumulative spending
5. **Unique codes**: Each transaction has unique ID
6. **No deletion**: Transaction data cannot be deleted (soft delete only)
7. **Audit trail**: All changes logged
8. **VNPay confirmation**: Orders confirmed after successful callback
9. **Cancellation reason**: Required for cancelled orders
10. **Inventory adjustment**: Must record who and when
